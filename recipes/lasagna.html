Lasagna Soup

What could be more comforting than lasagna? How about a big bowl of lasagna soup?

This easy, 25-minute recipe gives you all the flavor of the world's favorite casserole in the world's

favorite comfort-food form! Broken noodles simmer in a tomato broth with Italian sausage and spinach,

and the whole thing is capped off with a big dollop of blended cheese.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Yellow onion
  • Clove garlic
  • Salt and Black pepper
  • Sweet Sausages
  • Tomato paste
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot
  2. Add onions and garlic
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Cook and stir occasionnaly for 5 min
  5. Add sausages and cook
  6. Add tomato paste, oregano, and basil, and cook
  7. Add marinara and bring to boil
  8. Add noodles and cook until soft
