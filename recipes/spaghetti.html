Spaghetti With Meat Sauce

What is This Recipe?

It is an easy weeknight friendly spaghetti and meat sauce thatâ€™s

made completely from scratch. Use ground beef, turkey, pork,

chicken or lamb to make this simple spaghetti recipe.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Canned spaghetti meat sauce
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil water with pot
  2. Add salt to water
  3. Add spaghetti to pot when boilling
  4. Once soft, strain the spaghetti
  5. In another pan add the sauce
  6. Mix spaghetti with sauce and gently stir
  7. Add basil leaves on top
  8. Serve hot and enjoy
